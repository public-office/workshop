This livestream is an attempt to reach beyond what André Lepecki refers to as "chrono-normativity" of performance in measured time. Over the course of 24 hours, moments of performance will emerge and recede within a landscape of quotidian domestic scenarios. Through the course of the day, Wilmer will be at home studying and elaborating on the connections between the history of "durational" performance art, the rise of the feed as a ubiquitous social and cultural form, and the expansion of domestic life we are in the midst of via quarantine lockdown.
